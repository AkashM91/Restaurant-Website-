Desserts

Chocolate Brownie and Ice Cream

A brownie is a square baked dessert. We use nuts and chocolate chips to make them more substantial. We add ice cream to enhance the taste of the brownie.

Ice Creme Sundae

It is a sweet ice cream dessert. It has an option of one or more scoops of ice cream topped with hot fudged, whipped cream, and cherries.

Crepe

Crepes are especially popular throughout France. The ingredients include flour, eggs, milk, butter, and a pinch of salt. Our crepes come with an option of toppings. You can choose strawberries, bannanas, chocolate chips, ice cream, whip cream and cherries.

Waffles

The stiff batter for the waffles is made from flour, butter, brown sugar, yeast, milk, and eggs. On our waffles, we have a wide range of toppings. We have strawberries, bannanas, chocolate chips, ice cream, whip cream and cherries, oreos, and coockie crumble.

Chocolate Chip Cookie Bar

A chocolate chip cookie bar cookie is a type of cookie made by baking batter in a sheet pan, then cut into bars or squares.

Chocolate Mousse

A mousse is a soft prepared food that incorporates air bubbles to give it a light and airy texture. It can range from light and fluffy to creamy and thick. Our mousse is chocolat.
