Garden Salad

A garden salad consisting of lettuce, cucumber, scallions, cherry tomatoes, olives, sun-dried tomatoes, and cheese. Main ingredients. A base of vegetables, fruits, meat, eggs, or grains; mixed with our special sauce.

Brushetta

Bruschetta is dish from Italy consisting of grilled bread rubbed with garlic and topped with olive oil and salt. It includes toppings of tomato, vegetables and cheese.

Poutine

Poutine is a Quebecois dish popular across Canada, made with French fries and cheese curds topped with a light brown gravy.

Onion Rings

Onion rings are a form of appetizer or side dish commonly found in the United States, Canada, United Kingdom, and some parts of Asia and Continental Europe. They generally consist of a cross-sectional "ring" of onion dipped in batter or bread crumbs and then deep fried.

Falafel

Falafel or felafel is a deep-fried ball, doughnut or patty made from ground chickpeas. It is server with our homeade dips.

Baked Brie

Baked Brie is a dish that serves brie that has been freshly baked in the oven, which is then that is filled into pastry.
